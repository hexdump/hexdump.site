Projects

UMass BINDS

For the past year, I've been working with professor Ed Rietman at the UMass BINDS (Biologically-Inspired Neuro-Dynamical Systems) lab on reservoir computing on exotic and FPGA substrates.

Ergonomica

In my spare time, I work on a project called Ergonomica. Ergonomica is a Python-based shell that aims to provide a cohesive shell experience which draws inspiration from both traditional shells as well as modern programming language design. Also under the Ergonomica name, I'm currently developing a Python-based, minimal text editor.
